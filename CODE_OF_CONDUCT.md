# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful, constructive, and professional.
- Assume positive intent while reviewing code and design decisions.
- Focus on technical outcomes and user impact.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Personal attacks, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Harassment, trolling, or intentionally disruptive behavior.
- Sharing private or sensitive information without consent.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict participation for behavior that violates this code.

## Reporting

Open a private security/report issue or contact maintainers through repository security channels.
